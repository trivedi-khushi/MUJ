Why do you like open source?

Open to All: Open-source software breaks down barriers by making code freely available to anyone, regardless of financial resources or geographic location. This fosters a more inclusive and equitable tech landscape. Collective Intelligence: It unites developers and users from diverse backgrounds to contribute to a shared project. This collective effort often leads to more innovative, robust, and adaptable solutions. Accelerated Innovation: The open exchange of ideas and knowledge fuels rapid development and problem-solving. Features and fixes can be implemented quickly, as experts from around the globe collaborate.
